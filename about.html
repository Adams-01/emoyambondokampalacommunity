Our Journey

Established in 2019, Emo Ya Mbondo is the leading convener of African private sector leaders for social impact. We provide tailored solutions to the private sector, enabling them to tap into the African demographic dividend, including in vulnerable settings like displaced communities. We also empower the youth of Africa, including those in displaced communities, through strategic connections with the private sector to co-create breakthrough opportunities for all.

About Us

Emo Ya Mbondo is a nonprofitable organization dedicated to creating lasting change in all Babondo communities through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment programs.
